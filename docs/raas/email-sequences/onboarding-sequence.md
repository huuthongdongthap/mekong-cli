### Subject Lines

| Variant A | Variant B |

| "The 5-minute setup" | "How Sarah saved 12 hrs/week" |

| "14 days in" | "Your competitors are already" |

| Variant A | Variant B |
|-----------|-----------|
| "View Dashboard" | "See Your ROI" |
| "Browse Agents" | "Find Your Agent" |
| "Upgrade to Growth" | "Unlock Unlimited" |
| "Book a Demo" | "Claim Your Spot" |

### Send Time

- Test morning (9 AM) vs. afternoon (2 PM) vs. evening (7 PM)
- Test weekday (Tue-Thu) vs. weekend (Sat-Sun)

| Segment | Trigger | Email Adjustment |
|---------|---------|------------------|
| Active (daily login) | Dashboard opens daily | Skip Day 7 upgrade push, focus on scaling tips |
| Stuck (no agent deployed by Day 3) | 0 agents | Send extra hand-holding, offer onboarding call |
| Power User (3+ agents) | Deployed 3+ | Fast-track to Premium, highlight API |
| At-Risk (no login after Day 5) | Inactive | Trigger Day 30 win-back early |
