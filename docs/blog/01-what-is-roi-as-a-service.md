# What is ROI-as-a-Service (RaaS)?

## The Problem: AI Automation Without Accountability

Traditional AI implementations share a common flaw: **you pay upfront, hope for results, and rarely know the true ROI**. Companies invest thousands in AI tools, only to discover months later that adoption is low, workflows haven't changed, and the promised efficiency gains never materialized.

According to McKinsey, 70% of digital transformation initiatives fail to deliver expected returns. The gap isn't technology—it's **accountability**.

## Enter ROI-as-a-Service (RaaS)

**RaaS flips the model:** Instead of paying for software licenses, you pay for **measurable business outcomes**. Every AI interaction is tracked, every credit is accounted for, and every dollar spent ties directly to revenue generated or costs saved.

### Core Principles

| Principle | Traditional SaaS | RaaS |
|-----------|-----------------|-----|
| **Pricing** | Per-seat, per-month | Per-action, per-result |
| **Accountability** | Vendor provides tools | Vendor guarantees outcomes |
| **Transparency** | Usage dashboards | Real-time credit tracking |
| **Risk** | Customer bears all | Shared between parties |

## How RaaS Works

### 1. Credit-Based Economy

Every action in the RaaS ecosystem consumes credits:

```
Simple task (reply to email)     → 1 credit
Standard task (generate report)  → 3 credits
Complex task (multi-agent workflow) → 5 credits
```

Credits are purchased upfront and deducted in real-time. This creates a **direct economic link** between what you do and what you pay.

### 2. Transparent Metering

Every transaction is recorded on-chain (or in an immutable ledger):

- **Who** initiated the task
- **What** action was performed
- **When** it executed
- **How many** credits consumed
- **Result** achieved

This audit trail enables:
- Finance teams to reconcile AI spend
- Operations to identify high-ROI workflows
- Leadership to make data-driven automation decisions

### 3. Tiered Subscription + Credit Packs

RaaS offers flexibility:

| Tier | Monthly Price | Included Credits | Target |

| Free | $0 | 10 | Testing |
| Starter | $29 | 50 | Solo founders |
| Pro | $99 | 200 | Small teams |
| Agency | $199 | 500 | Growing agencies |

Additional credits available à la carte:

2. **Approval workflows** for high-value transactions
3. **Role-based access** ensures proper authorization
4. **Compliance logging** for regulated industries
