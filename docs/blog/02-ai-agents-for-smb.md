## What Are AI Agents?

AI agents are **autonomous software workers** that:

1. **Perceive**: Read inputs (emails, tickets, documents, APIs)
2. **Execute**: Plan and take actions (reply, create, update, notify)
3. **Verify**: Check results and report outcomes
4. **Iterate**: Improve based on feedback

Unlike chatbots that wait for prompts, agents **initiate work** toward goals you define.

## Common Pitfalls (And How to Avoid Them)

### ❌ Pitfall 1: Boiling the Ocean
**Mistake:** Automating everything at once
**Fix:** Start with ONE task. Master it. Then expand.

### ❌ Pitfall 2: No Human Oversight
**Mistake:** Set-and-forget agents
**Fix:** Weekly reviews. Approval gates for high-value actions.

### ❌ Pitfall 3: Ignoring Credit Economics
**Mistake:** Running agents without monitoring spend
**Fix:** Set daily credit limits. Get alerts at 80% threshold.

### ❌ Pitfall 4: Poor Goal Definition
**Mistake:** Vague instructions like "handle support"
**Fix:** Specific goals: "Reply to all tier-1 support tickets within 5 minutes using KB articles"
