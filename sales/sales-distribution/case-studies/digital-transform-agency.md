## Key Takeaways

### What Worked

✅ **Start with low-risk missions:** Password reset, simple CRUD
✅ **Train the team early:** 2-hour workshop on mission writing
✅ **Establish approval gates:** Code review before production
✅ **Track metrics weekly:** Credit usage, mission success rate
✅ **White-label for clients:** They see your brand, not RaaS

### What They'd Do Differently

📝 **Start sooner:** "We waited 6 months too long. Wish we started in Q4."
📝 **More aggressive rollout:** Could have gone faster than 90 days
📝 **Earlier enterprise pitch:** Could have charged more for faster delivery
