## The Problem

**Hourly billing is broken.**

- **Clients** hate unpredictable costs and paying for inefficiency
- **Agencies** are capped by human capacity and penalized for efficiency
- **Result:** Adversarial relationships, constant scope fights, capped growth

## The Solution: RaaS

**Revenue-as-a-Service (RaaS)** transforms how agencies deliver and bill for development work.

| Before (Hourly) | After (RaaS) |
|-----------------|--------------|
| Bill for hours worked | Bill for outcomes delivered |
| Client manages tasks | AI agents execute autonomously |
| Scale = hire more humans | Scale = deploy more agents |
| Revenue capped by capacity | Revenue scales with demand |

## How It Works

**3-Step Mission Execution:**

1. **Client submits mission:** "Add user authentication with OAuth2"
2. **AI agent team executes:** Planner → Coder → Tester → Reviewer
3. **Mission complete:** Code written, tested, deployed. Credits deducted. ROI tracked.

**Result:** What used to take 4 hours now takes 15 minutes.
