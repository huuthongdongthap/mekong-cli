## Round Sizing Guide

| Stage | Typical Range | Dilution | Duration |
|-------|---------------|----------|----------|
| Pre-seed | $50K-$500K | 5-10% | 2-4 weeks |
| Seed | $500K-$3M | 15-25% | 4-8 weeks |
| Series A | $3M-$15M | 20-30% | 8-16 weeks |
| Series B | $15M-$50M | 15-25% | 8-12 weeks |

## Fundraising Timeline

```
Week -4: Prep materials (deck, data room, financials)
Week -2: Warm introductions to 30-50 investors
Week  0: First meetings begin (batch them!)
Week  2: Follow-up meetings, deep dives
Week  4: Term sheets arrive
Week  5: Negotiate terms, select lead
Week  6: Due diligence
Week  8: Close round, wire funds
```

## Instrument Guide

| Instrument | Best For | Key Terms |
|-----------|----------|-----------|
| SAFE | Pre-seed, Seed | Valuation cap, discount |
| Convertible Note | Seed | Interest rate, cap, maturity |
| Priced Round | Series A+ | Valuation, liquidation pref |

### SAFE Quick Calculator
```
Investment: $500K
Cap: $5M
Discount: 20%

If Series A at $10M pre-money:
  → SAFE converts at $5M cap (better for investor)
  → Investor gets: $500K / $5M = 10% ownership
```

- [ ] Cap table (current + pro forma)

- [ ] IP assignments
