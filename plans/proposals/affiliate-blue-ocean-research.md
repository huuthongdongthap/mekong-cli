## ⚠️ Key Considerations

### Payout Terms Summary

| Term        | Programs                                 |
| ----------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **Net 15**  | Rare (negotiate with high volume)        |
| **Net 30**  | Most programs (CoinLedger, Jasper, etc.) |
| **Net 45**  | Some (CyberGhost, PicMonkey)             |
| **Net 60+** | Avoid                                    |

### Cookie Duration Winners

| Cookie       | Programs                                |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------- |
| **Lifetime** | Koinly, CoinPanda, Senja, Symlex VPN    |
| **365 days** | Pabbly                                  |
| **120 days** | FreshBooks, Gusto, GetResponse, Semrush |
| **90 days**  | HubSpot, Thinkific, Teachable, Kinsta   |

### Red Flags to Avoid

- ❌ Programs với minimum payout > $100
- ❌ Cookie duration < 30 days
- ❌ One-time commission only (prefer recurring)
- ❌ Crypto exchange affiliate (high regulatory risk)
- ❌ Unclear payout terms
