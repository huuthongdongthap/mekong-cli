# Algo-Trader Sales Playbook

**Product:** AI-powered crypto arbitrage engine
**Target:** Institutional traders ($10M+ AUM)
**Pricing:** $2K-$50K/mo + 10% performance fee
**Sales Cycle:** 4-8 weeks (enterprise)

## 6-STAGE SALES PROCESS

### Stage 1: Lead Generation (Week 0-1)

**Target Profile (ICP):**
| Criteria | Specification |
|----------|---------------|
| AUM | $10M+ (minimum), $50M+ (ideal) |
| Entity Type | Family offices, crypto hedge funds, prop trading firms, RIAs |
| Geography | US, EU, Singapore, UAE (crypto-friendly) |
| Current Stack | Already using TradingView, Bloomberg, or similar |
| Pain Signals | Tweeting about "missing opportunities", "manual trading is slow" |

**Lead Sources:**
| Channel | Volume | Conversion | Cost/Lead |
|---------|--------|------------|-----------|
| LinkedIn (founder outreach) | 50/week | 3-5% | $0 |
| Crypto Twitter (DM) | 30/week | 2-3% | $0 |
| Industry events (Permissionless, Consensus) | 20/event | 10-15% | $5K/event |
| Partnerships (custodians, prime brokers) | 5/month | 20-30% | Revenue share |
| Content (substack, youtube) | Inbound | 5-10% | $2K/mo production |

**Exit Criteria:** 50+ leads in pipeline, 10+ first meetings booked

### Stage 2: Qualification — BANT Framework (Week 1-2)

**BANT Scorecard (25 points total, 18+ to proceed):**

| Criterion | 5 pts | 3 pts | 1 pt |
|-----------|-------|-------|------|
| **Budget** | $10K+/mo confirmed | $5K-10K/mo discussed | <$5K or undefined |
| **Authority** | Decision maker | Influencer | Gatekeeper |
| **Need** | Active pain, competing solutions | Exploring solutions | Curiosity only |
| **Timeline** | <30 days | 30-90 days | 90+ days |
| **AUM** | $100M+ | $25-100M | $10-25M |

**Disqualify Immediately If:**
- AUM <$10M (can't afford)
- No crypto trading experience (onboarding cost too high)
- "Just researching" with no timeline
- Expecting 100%+ returns (unrealistic expectations)

**Discovery Call Script (30 min):**
```
1. Opener (2 min)
   "Thanks for making time. I want to understand your current trading setup
   and see if we can help. If not, I'll tell you upfront."

2. Current State (10 min)
   - "Walk me through your current trading workflow"
   - "How do you find arbitrage opportunities today?"
   - "What's your monthly volume in crypto?"
   - "How much slippage do you estimate on manual trades?"

3. Pain Quantification (8 min)
   - "What's the cost of missing a major arb opportunity?"
   - "How many hours/week does your team spend on manual execution?"
   - "What happens when you're asleep and opportunities appear?"

4. Solution Fit (5 min)
   - Show 2-min demo clip (pre-recorded)
   - "This is what automated execution looks like. Does this match your needs?"

5. Next Steps (5 min)
   - If qualified: "Let me show you a live demo tailored to your strategy"
   - If unsure: "I'll send you a case study. Let's reconnect in 2 weeks"
   - If not fit: "I don't think we're the right fit. Here's a cheaper alternative"
```

**Exit Criteria:** BANT 18+, demo scheduled within 5 days

### Stage 3: Demo (Week 2-3)

**30-Minute Demo Flow:**

| Time | Section | Key Moment |
|------|---------|------------|
| 0-3 min | Setup & context | "Show me your current PnL tracking" |
| 3-8 min | Live arb scan | **Moment 1:** Show real-time opportunities they're missing |
| 8-15 min | Execution demo | **Moment 2:** One-click vs manual comparison |
| 15-20 min | Risk controls | "What's your max position size? Let me show you the circuit breaker" |
| 20-25 min | PnL analytics | Show projected ROI based on their volume |
| 25-30 min | Trial close | "Does this solve the problem we discussed?" |

**Demo Environment:**
- Use their exchange accounts (testnet) if possible
- Pre-load with their typical trading pairs
- Show side-by-side: manual vs algo execution

**Key Moments (Must Hit):**
1. **"You're missing $X/day"** — Calculate based on their volume
2. **"This would have caught [recent event]"** — Reference a specific arb they missed
3. **"Your competitor uses this"** — Social proof (if allowed to share)

**Objection Handling:**
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We have devs building this" | "How's that going? What's the timeline? What's the opportunity cost?" |
| "Too expensive" | "What's the cost of missing one 5% arb? This pays for itself in X trades" |
| "Security concerns" | "We never hold funds. API keys are read-only + trade-only. Here's our security doc" |
| "Need to think about it" | "What specifically? Let me address that now" |

**Exit Criteria:** Trial signup OR "not a fit" (no more than 2 follow-up demos)

### Stage 4: Trial — 14-Day Onboarding (Week 3-5)

**Day 0: Setup (2 hours)**
- [ ] API keys configured (testnet first)
- [ ] Trading pairs selected
- [ ] Risk limits set (max position, daily loss limit)
- [ ] Slack/Discord webhook connected
- [ ] Welcome email sent

**Day 1-3: Observation Mode**
- Bot scans only, no execution
- Daily report: "You would have made $X on N opportunities"
- Check-in call on Day 2

**Day 4-7: Small Size Execution**
- Enable live trading at 10% position size
- Monitor fill rates, slippage
- Slack alerts for every trade
- Check-in call on Day 5

**Day 8-14: Full Size**
- Increase to 50-100% position size
- Compare PnL vs manual baseline
- Day 10: Mid-trial review call
- Day 13: "Decision conversation" scheduled

**Trial Success Metrics:**
| Metric | Target | Alert Threshold |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Opportunities caught | >80% | <60% |
| Avg slippage | <0.5% | >1% |
| Fill rate | >90% | <75% |
| PnL vs manual | +20%+ | <-10% |

**Trial → Paid Conversion: 60-70%**

**Exit Criteria:** Contract sent OR trial ended (no conversion)

### Stage 5: Negotiation (Week 5-7)

**Pricing Tiers:**

| Tier | Monthly | Setup | Performance Fee | Target AUM |
|------|---------|-------|-----------------|------------|
| Starter | $2,000 | $5,000 | 10% | $10-25M |
| Professional | $5,000 | $10,000 | 10% | $25-50M |
| Institutional | $15,000 | $25,000 | 10% | $50-100M |
| Enterprise | $50,000 | Custom | 10% | $100M+ |

**Note:** All tiers include standard 10% performance fee. Performance fee based on backtested results, not live performance. SEC filing required.

**When to Offer Which Tier:**
- **Starter:** First-time crypto traders, small family offices
- **Professional:** Established prop shops, multi-strat funds
- **Institutional:** Hedge funds, multiple trading desks (~20 clients needed for $1M ARR)
- **Enterprise:** Market makers, institutions needing custom integrations

**Revenue Target Note:** $1M ARR requires ~20 Institutional clients at $15K/mo average, or equivalent mix across tiers.

**Negotiation Scripts:**

*Discount Request:*
```
Prospect: "Can you do $3K instead of $5K?"

You: "I understand budget constraints. Let me show you the ROI:
     At your volume, you're missing ~$50K/month in arb opportunities.
     Even at $5K, that's 10x return. What would make this a no-brainer?"

Fallback: "I can do $4K if you sign a 6-month prepay. That's the best I can do
          without removing features."
```

*Performance Fee Pushback:*
```
Prospect: "10% performance fee is too high."

You: "Fair. We only make money when you make money. That's alignment.
     What's your current profit share structure with other partners?"

Fallback: "I can reduce to 8% if we increase the base to $7K. That gives you
          more upside protection."
```

*Competitor Mention:*
```
Prospect: "HaasOnline is cheaper."

You: "They are. They also focus on retail. We're built for institutional size.
     What's your average trade size? [Wait] At that size, slippage alone costs
     more than our fee difference."
```

**Exit Criteria:** Verbal agreement, contract under review

### FAQ (Sales Objections)

**Q: What if the algo makes a bad trade?**
A: The algo doesn't "make trades" — it executes pre-defined strategies.
   You set the parameters. It's a tool, not a black box.

**Q: How do you handle exchange outages?**
A: Auto-failover to backup APIs. If an exchange goes dark, positions
   freeze but you're not exposed. We alert immediately.

**Q: Can we customize the strategies?**
A: Yes. The engine ships with 400+ pre-built strategies. Your team can
   modify or create new ones via our strategy editor.

**Q: What's your uptime SLA?**
A: 99.9% uptime guarantee. If we miss, you get pro-rated credit.
   We've had 12 minutes of downtime in 18 months.

**Q: Do you take custody of funds?**
A: Never. Funds stay on your exchanges. We only have API keys with
   trade-only permissions (no withdrawal).
