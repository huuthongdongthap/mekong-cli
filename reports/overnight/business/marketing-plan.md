# Marketing Plan — Mekong CLI

## Overview
Mekong CLI targets developers, founders, and agencies who want AI to *execute* business tasks — not just assist. Marketing strategy is PLG-first (Product-Led Growth) with content amplification and community-driven distribution.

---

## Target Personas

| Persona | Pain | Mekong Hook |
|---------|------|-------------|
| Solo developer | Context-switching between coding and ops | `mekong cook` replaces a PM |
| Technical founder | Drowning in sales/finance/HR overhead | 289 commands cover entire company ops |
| Agency owner | Client delivery bottlenecks, high labor cost | White-label AI ops, `mekong deploy` |
| Non-tech founder | Can't code, can't afford devs | `mekong annual "Kế hoạch 2026"` works |

---

## Marketing Strategy: PLG + Content + Community

### Phase 1 — Acquisition (Month 1-3)
**Goal:** 1,000 GitHub stars, 500 pip installs/wk

Channels:
- **Hacker News**: "Show HN: I built a CLI that runs your entire business" post
- **Dev.to / Hashnode**: Tutorial series — "Replace your SaaS stack with 3 env vars"
- **Reddit**: r/Python, r/MachineLearning, r/Entrepreneur posts
- **Twitter/X**: Developer community engagement, demo GIFs of `mekong cook`
- **GitHub**: Trending via star campaigns, quality README with viral hook

Primary commands: `mekong social`, `mekong seo`, `mekong content`

### Phase 2 — Activation (Month 3-6)
**Goal:** 5% free→paid conversion, 100 paying customers

Channels:
- **Email drip**: Post-install onboarding sequence (Polar.sh + email)
- **YouTube**: Loom-style demos — "Watch AI close a sales deal in 3 min"
- **Product Hunt**: Launch with community upvote campaign
- **LinkedIn**: Founder stories, before/after AI ops transformation

Primary commands: `mekong campaign`, `mekong marketing-campaign-run`, `mekong email`

### Phase 3 — Revenue (Month 6-12)
**Goal:** $50K MRR, 500 paid customers

Channels:
- **Partnership co-marketing**: OpenRouter, Ollama, DeepSeek integrations
- **Affiliate program**: 20% commission (`mekong affiliate`)
- **Case studies**: "Agency 3x'd throughput with Mekong CLI"
- **Conference talks**: PyCon, AI engineer summits

Primary commands: `mekong marketing-plan`, `mekong partnerships`, `mekong marketing-performance-report`
