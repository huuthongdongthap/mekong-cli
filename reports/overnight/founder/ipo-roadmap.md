# Long-Term IPO Roadmap — Mekong CLI

**Date:** March 2026
**Covers:** ipo/*, founder-ipo
**Horizon:** 2026–2032 (6-year path)

---

## IPO Thesis

Mekong CLI is building toward the first AI-native business operating system IPO from Southeast Asia. The structural advantages — 92% gross margins, $0 infrastructure cost on Cloudflare, universal LLM architecture, and the only 5-layer business platform — create a defensible path to $100M ARR, the minimum threshold for a credible tech IPO.

**Target IPO window:** 2031–2032
**Target ARR at IPO:** $80–120M
**Target market cap at IPO:** $800M–$2B (8–15x ARR multiple, SaaS norms)
**Exchange:** NASDAQ (primary) or SGX (secondary/dual listing)

## IPO Day Execution (`mekong founder-ipo/ipo-day`)

The `ipo-day` command orchestrates the entire IPO day workflow:

```bash
mekong founder-ipo/ipo-day --exchange NASDAQ --ticker MEKO
```

Automated tasks on IPO day:
1. Press release distribution (BusinessWire + local VN media)
2. Social media posts timed to market open
3. Employee communication (all hands message)
4. Investor relations page live on agencyos.network
5. Stock ticker widget embedded in landing page
6. Celebration trigger: Tôm Hùm sends congratulations to all customers
7. First-day trading monitoring dashboard
