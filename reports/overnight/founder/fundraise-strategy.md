# Fundraise Strategy — Mekong CLI Seed Round

**Date:** March 2026
**Covers:** fundraise, founder-raise, seed, data-room, investor-update
**Target:** $500K–$1M seed round, Q4 2026

---

## Fundraise Thesis

Mekong CLI is the only open-source AI platform covering all 5 business layers with a live billing system, 289 commands, and a PEV engine that self-heals and rolls back on failure. We are raising to hire 2 people and accelerate the agent marketplace flywheel.

**We raise when:** MRR > $10,000 (strong negotiating position). Target: October 2026.

---

## Why Raise (and Why Not)

### Arguments FOR raising

- Agent marketplace requires engineering bandwidth beyond solo founder + AI agents
- Enterprise sales motion (legal, security review, procurement) needs human presence
- LLM routing infrastructure needs hardening for 99.9% SLA
- Distribution: $50K in paid ads could 10x trial installs during a critical window

### Arguments AGAINST raising (risks)

- Dilution at seed is expensive — $500K at $3M post = 16.7% gone
- Investor pressure to hire fast, often wrong hires
- Reporting overhead costs founder bandwidth
- Current burn is $570/mo — breakeven at 10 customers, no urgency

**Decision:** Raise only after $10K MRR proves PMF. Use raise to accelerate, not survive.
