# Paid Ads Analysis — Mekong CLI

**Question:** Do dev tools need paid ads? | **Answer:** Not at this stage.

---

## Analysis

### Unit Economics Reality Check

| Channel | Estimated CAC | ARPU | Payback Period | Viable? |
|---------|--------------|------|---------------|---------|
| Google Search Ads | $80–$200 | $49 (Starter) | Never (negative) | No |
| Twitter/X Ads | $50–$150 | $49 | Never | No |
| Reddit Ads | $40–$120 | $49 | Never | No |
| Newsletter sponsorship | $20–$50 | $49 | Month 1–2 | Maybe |
| Dev.to sponsored post | $15–$40 | $49 | Month 1 | Maybe |

At $49 ARPU and 75% gross margin ($36.75 gross profit), CAC must be under $30 for a 1-month payback. Only content sponsorships approach viability.

**Conclusion: Paid ads are not viable until Pro ($149) becomes the primary tier.**

---

## When Paid Ads Become Viable

| Condition | Why It Changes the Math |
|-----------|------------------------|
| ARPU reaches $100+ (Pro dominant) | CAC < $50 becomes profitable |
| LTV proven at 12+ months | Justify $100 CAC if LTV = $1,200 |
| Conversion rate tested and known | Can calculate max bid accurately |
| $5K+ MRR with margin to reinvest | Cash flow supports ad spend |

**Earliest viable timeline for paid ads:** Q4 2026, targeting Pro tier only.

## Channel Assessments

### Google Search Ads
- **Keyword:** "AI business CLI", "AI automation tool for founders"
- **CPC estimate:** $3–$8/click
- **Conversion to trial:** ~2% (cold traffic, developer skepticism)
- **CAC:** $150–$400
- **Verdict:** Not viable. Keywords too expensive, conversion too low.

### Reddit Ads
- **Targeting:** r/SideProject, r/MachineLearning, r/Python subscribers
- **CPM:** $5–$15
- **Conversion:** Low (Reddit users distrust ads heavily)
- **Verdict:** Not viable. Organic posts outperform ads on Reddit.

### Twitter/X Promoted Posts
- **Best use:** Boost a high-performing organic tweet
- **CPC:** $0.50–$2.00
- **Conversion:** Unknown without testing
- **Verdict:** Test only after $3K MRR with $100 experiment budget.

### Newsletter Sponsorships (Best Option)
| Newsletter | Audience | Cost/Issue | Est. Clicks | Est. CAC |
|-----------|---------|-----------|------------|---------|
| TLDR Dev | 500K devs | $2,000 | 500–1,500 | $8–$25 |
| Bytes.dev | 200K React devs | $800 | 200–600 | $8–$25 |
| Pointer.io | 15K eng leaders | $400 | 50–150 | $15–$50 |

Newsletter is the most viable paid channel. Test with one TLDR issue at $3K MRR.
