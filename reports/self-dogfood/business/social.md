# Social Media Strategy — Mekong CLI

**Channels:** Twitter/X (primary), LinkedIn (secondary) | **Budget:** $0

---

## Channel Strategy

### Twitter/X — Primary Channel

**Why:** Developer Twitter is where OSS tools get discovered. @levelsio, @patio11, @swyx, @t3dotgg audiences are our ICP. One viral tweet > 1,000 Dev.to readers.

**Account positioning:** Founder-led, build-in-public, honest metrics sharing

**Content mix:**
| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Build-in-public updates | 3×/week | Authenticity, community |
| Technical threads | 1×/week | Credibility, SEO |
| Demo clips (asciinema GIFs) | 1×/week | Product discovery |
| Honest founder takes | 2×/week | Personality, shareability |
| Milestone announcements | As happens | Social proof |
| Engagement (replies) | Daily | Relationship building |

**Posting schedule:** 9am–12pm EST (peak developer Twitter hours)

---

### LinkedIn — Secondary Channel

**Why:** Agency owners and startup team leads (Secondary ICP) live on LinkedIn. Different audience than Twitter — more business-minded, less technically deep.

**Content mix:**
| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Founder story posts | 1×/week | Brand building |
| Business results (when available) | Monthly | Credibility |
| Repurposed Twitter threads | 1×/week | Low-effort distribution |
| Industry commentary | 1×/week | Thought leadership |

## What NOT to Do

- No promotional tweets without value ("Check out Mekong CLI!")
- No buying followers or engagement pods
- No cross-posting identical content to all channels
- No posting more than 3× per day (quality over quantity)
- No engaging with drama or negativity
