## Outreach Priority Scoring

Calculate for each lead:

```
Score = (Revenue Fit × 0.3) + (Industry Match × 0.25) +
        (Pain Point Severity × 0.25) + (Trigger Event × 0.2)

Priority:
- 80-100: Tier 1 (Outreach Week 1-2)
- 60-79: Tier 2 (Outreach Week 3-4)
- 40-59: Tier 3 (Outreach Month 2)
- <40: Nurture Campaign
```
