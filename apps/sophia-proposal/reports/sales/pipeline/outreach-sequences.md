## Objection Handling Scripts

### "Budget is tight"

```
"I get it — Q2 budgets are locked in. Two options:

1. Start with Starter ($499/mo) for 30-day proof. If you don't see
   3x ROAS, no obligation to continue.

2. We can do a pilot: 50 videos for one product line. Prove ROI
   first, then expand.

Which makes more sense for [Company]?"
```

### "We have an agency"

```
"Love that you're invested in quality creative. Most of our customers
kept their agency and used us for:

- Volume: Agency does 5 hero videos/mo, we do 50 product videos
- Speed: 90-second turnaround for trending hooks
- Testing: 20 variations per concept vs. 1 agency video

Think of us as your agency's production partner, not a replacement.

Worth exploring how we could supplement [Agency Name]?"
```

### "AI videos look generic"

```
"Fair concern — early AI video tools were cookie-cutter. Here's what's
different:

1. E-commerce formula: Trained on 1,247 high-ROAS D2C ads, not
   generic templates

2. Brand customization: Upload your brand kit (colors, fonts, tone)
   — outputs match your identity

3. Product-first: Pulls actual product imagery + copy from your URL,
   not stock assets

Here's [Similar Brand]'s video — tell me if this looks generic:
[Video Link]

What specifically concerns you about quality?"
```

### "Need to talk to [boss/board]"

```
"Absolutely — big decisions should be collaborative.

Here's what I recommend:
1. I'll send a 1-pager you can share internally
2. Include the ROI calc specific to [Company]
3. Offer to join that conversation to answer technical questions

Who else needs to be in the room? And when are you presenting?"
```

### "Send me more info"

```
"Happy to! To make it relevant, quick question:

What's the #1 thing you'd need to see to move forward with a video
production solution?

- ROI case studies from similar brands?
- Technical integration details?
- Pricing breakdown?
- Something else?

I'll tailor the info to what matters most to [Company]."
```

### A/B Testing Framework

**Test One Variable Per Send:**

| Variable | Variant A | Variant B |
|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Subject Line | Question | Statement |
| Length | <100 words | >200 words |
| CTA | Calendar link | "Reply yes" |
| Social Proof | Logo | Stat |
| Send Time | Tuesday 10am | Thursday 2pm |

**Sample Size:** Minimum 50 sends per variant before declaring winner
