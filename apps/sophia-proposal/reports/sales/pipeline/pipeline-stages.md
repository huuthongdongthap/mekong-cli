## Stage 2: Qualification (25% Probability)

### Entry Criteria
- [ ] Lead responded to outreach
- [ ] Initial interest expressed

### BANT Framework

#### Budget
- **Question:** "What's your current monthly ad creative budget?"
- **Qualifying:** $20K+/mo ad spend, $500-2K/mo creative budget
- **Disqualifying:** No budget allocated, < $5K/mo spend

#### Authority
- **Question:** "Who else is involved in selecting creative production tools?"
- **Qualifying:** Decision maker or strong influencer
- **Disqualifying:** No budget authority, complex committee

#### Need
- **Question:** "What's your biggest challenge with video creative production?"
- **Qualifying:** Creative fatigue, agency bottlenecks, ROAS declining
- **Disqualifying:** Happy with current solution, no pain point

#### Timeline
- **Question:** "When are you looking to solve this?"
- **Qualifying:** Q2/Q3 planning, new campaign upcoming
- **Disqualifying:** "Someday", no urgency

### Exit Criteria (→ Discovery Demo)
- [ ] BANT score ≥ 7/10
- [ ] Demo meeting booked
- [ ] Calendar invite sent

### Metrics
- **Conversion Rate:** 50% → Discovery Demo
- **Velocity:** 5 days avg.

## Stage 5: Negotiation (85% Probability)

### Common Objections + Responses

#### "Budget is tight this quarter"
```
Response: "Understand. What if we start with Starter tier ($499/mo) for 30-day proof?
If you don't see 3x ROAS in first month, no obligation to continue."

Alternative: "We can do quarterly prepay ($1,347 = 10% discount) to fit budget cycle."
```

#### "Need to talk to [boss/board]"
```
Response: "Absolutely. Here's a 1-pager you can share internally:
- Problem: [their pain point]
- Solution: Sophia AI URL-to-video
- ROI: $X savings, Yx ROAS
- Investment: $Z/mo
Can I join that call to answer technical questions?"
```

#### "Contract length concern"
```
Response: "We offer monthly, quarterly (10% off), or annual (20% off).
Most customers start monthly, upgrade after seeing results.
No long-term lock-in required."
```

#### "Integration looks complex"
```
Response: "30-minute Shopify integration. Here's the step-by-step guide.
We also offer white-glove onboarding for Premium+ tiers."
```
