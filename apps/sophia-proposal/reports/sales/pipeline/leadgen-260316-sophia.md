## 6. Outreach Strategy

### Email Sequence (5-Touch)

**Touch 1 (Day 1)**: Personalized observation
```
Subject: Quick question about [Company]'s video workflow

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] is producing [X type] content for clients.
Impressive work on [specific project].

Quick question: How are you currently handling [specific pain point]?

Curious to learn more about your process.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Touch 2 (Day 3)**: Value add
```
Subject: Idea for [Company]

Hi [Name],

We helped [Similar Company] cut video production time by 60%
while maintaining quality.

Thought this might be relevant given [specific observation].

Happy to share the playbook if useful.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Touch 3 (Day 7)**: Social proof
```
Subject: [Similar Agency] case study

Hi [Name],

[Similar Agency] was spending $15K/mo on freelance editors.

After switching to Sophia AI:
- 70% cost reduction
- 3x faster turnaround
- Consistent quality

Worth a 15-min chat to see if this applies to [Company]?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Touch 4 (Day 14)**: Breakup
```
Subject: Last try

Hi [Name],

Don't want to flood your inbox.

If video automation isn't a priority right now, no problem.

Feel free to reach out when timing is better.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### LinkedIn Outreach

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Connect with personalized note |
| 2 | Engage with their content (like/comment) |
| 3 | Send value-add message (case study) |
| 4 | Request 15-min discovery call |
