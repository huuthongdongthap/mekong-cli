## 📜 13 Chapters Framework

### 1️⃣ Kế Hoạch (Planning)
**Assessment Checklist - Ngũ Sự:**
- **Đạo**: Are interests aligned across all stakeholders?
- **Thiên**: Is the timing right for this move?
- **Địa**: Is our positioning optimal?
- **Tướng**: Is leadership clear and capable?
- **Pháp**: Are processes standardized?

Use when: Starting new ventures, major decisions, quarterly planning

### 2️⃣ Tác Chiến (Resource Management)
**Runway Analysis:**
- Calculate burn rate and runway
- Identify resource constraints
- Optimize allocation

Use when: Financial planning, budget allocation, runway concerns

### 3️⃣ Mưu Công (Strategic Attack)
**Win Without Fighting:**
- Identify opportunities for bloodless victories
- Build alliances over adversaries
- Convert competitors to partners

Use when: Competitive situations, market entry, M&A

### 4️⃣ Hình Thế (Positioning)
**Competitive Moat Analysis:**
- Identify and strengthen moats
- Analyze competitor weaknesses
- Build sustainable advantages

Use when: Market positioning, differentiation, moat building

### 5️⃣ Thế Trận (Momentum)
**Network Effects & Growth:**
- Identify momentum opportunities
- Build compounding advantages
- Leverage network effects

Use when: Growth planning, scaling, network effects

Use when: Speed-to-market, competitive races, time-sensitive opportunities
