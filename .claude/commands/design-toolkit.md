## Branding 101 Checklist

- [ ] **Name & Logo**: Simple, works in monochrome and small sizes
- [ ] **Color Palette**: 1 Primary, 1 Secondary, 3 Neutrals, 3 Semantic (Red/Green/Yellow)
- [ ] **Typography**: 1 font for Headings (e.g. Inter), 1 for Body (e.g. Roboto)
- [ ] **Voice & Tone**: Define 3 adjectives (e.g. "Professional but approachable")
- [ ] **Brand Asset Folder**: Shareable Google Drive with SVG/PNG logos
