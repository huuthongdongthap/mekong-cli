## Is an Accelerator Right For You?

**✅ EXACTLY what you need if:**
- You are a first-time founder lacking network
- You need a deadline (Demo Day) to force momentum
- You need help navigating the fundraising landscape
- You are pre-PMF or just hitting early PMF

**❌ NOT for you if:**
- You already have strong investor networks
- You have high revenue/profitability (don't give up 7%!)
- Your business is a lifestyle/service business (not venture-scale)
- You can't commit full-time for 3-4 months

## Mastering the Application (The YC Format)

Almost all accelerators use variations of YC's questions. Here's how to answer them:

### "What is your company going to make?"
*Formula: What it is + Who it's for + What problem it solves*
- ❌ Bad: "An AI-powered synergy platform for revolutionary b2b metrics."
- ✅ Good: "We build automated payroll software for construction companies who currently use Excel."

### "What are you working on?" (The 50-word pitch)
- Be concise. Use plain English. No jargon. If your parents don't understand it, rewrite it.

### "Why did you choose this idea?"
- They are looking for **Founder-Market Fit** and **Unique Insight**.
- Explain the secret you know about this market that others don't.

### "How do or will you make money?"
- Be specific about unit economics. 
- "We charge $X per month per user."

## Interview Prep (10-Minute Drill)

Interviews are extremely fast (usually 10 mins). You must be concise.

**Prepare 1-sentence answers for:**
1. What do you do?
2. Who needs this?
3. How do you know they need it?
4. How are they solving it today?
5. Why is your solution better?
6. How will you acquire customers?
7. What is your unfair advantage?

*Rule: Answer the direct question immediately. Then add 1 compelling fact. Then STOP talking.*
