## Top 5 Growth Levers (The "Bullseye" Framework)

*Gabriel Weinberg's framework for finding your core channel:*
1. **Brainstorm** every possible traction channel
2. **Rank** them (Inner ring: high potential, Middle: promising, Outer: long-shots)
3. **Test** 3 channels from the inner ring simultaneously
4. **Focus** 100% on the single channel that works best 
5. **Scale** until the channel is exhausted, then repeat

## Tactics by Startup Stage

### Pre-Launch (0 users)
- **Waitlist Gamification**: Use a viral queue (invite X friends to move up)
- **Building in Public**: Share your journey on Twitter/X and IndieHackers
- **"Engineering as Marketing"**: Build a small free tool related to your core product
- **Warm Outreach**: Personally message 100 ideal customers on LinkedIn
- **Community Seeding**: Answer questions in relevant Reddit/Facebook groups

### Launch (0 → 1,000 users)
- **Product Hunt Blitz**: Coordinate launch with makers and early community
- **AppSumo/LTD Strategy**: Offer a lifetime deal to get initial cash + user feedback
- **Niche Influencers**: Sponsor small newsletters/creators (high ROI, low cost)
- **Aggregator Sites**: Submit to BetaList, HackerNews, and 50+ startup directories
- **The "Do Things That Don't Scale" Phase**: Hand-onboard every single user

### Scaling (1,000 → 100,000+ users)
- **Programmatic SEO**: Auto-generate thousands of landing pages based on data
- **Viral Loops**: Build sharing natively into the product (e.g., "Sent via iPhone")
- **Referral Programs**: Double-sided rewards (Give $10, Get $10)
- **Partnerships/Integrations**: Integrate with larger platforms to access their users
- **B2B Cold Email Engine**: Automated, highly personalized outbound at scale

## 6 "Built-in" Viral Loops

1. **The Network Effect**: Product is better when friends use it (WhatsApp, Slack)
2. **The Collaboration Loop**: Inviting others is required for a feature (Google Docs)
3. **The Value Loop**: Sharing creates value for the sender (Dropbox "Get more space")
4. **The Ego Loop**: Badges/status you want to show off (Strava runs, Github squares)
5. **The Signature Loop**: "Powered by [X]" on free products (Mailchimp, Intercom)
6. **The Financial Loop**: Affiliate or rev-share programs (Amazon Associates)
