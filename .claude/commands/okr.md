## AARRR Pirate Metrics

```
Acquisition → How do users find you?
Activation  → Do they have a great first experience?
Retention   → Do they come back?
Revenue     → Do they pay?
Referral    → Do they tell others?
```

## North Star Metric by Business Type

| Business Type | North Star Metric |
|---------------|-------------------|
| SaaS | Weekly Active Users or MRR |
| Marketplace | Transactions per week |
| E-commerce | Purchases per month |
| Media | Daily reading time |
| Social | Daily active users |
| Fintech | Assets under management |

## OKR Templates by Stage

### Pre-Seed (0-6 months)
```yaml
Objective: Validate problem-solution fit
  KR1: Conduct 50 customer interviews (target: 50)
  KR2: Build MVP in 4 weeks (target: 1 MVP)
  KR3: Get 100 waitlist signups (target: 100)

Objective: Find first 10 paying customers
  KR1: Convert 10% of beta users (target: 10%)
  KR2: Achieve $1K MRR (target: $1,000)
  KR3: NPS score ≥ 50 (target: 50)
```

### Seed (6-18 months)
```yaml
Objective: Achieve product-market fit
  KR1: 40% "very disappointed" on Sean Ellis (target: 40%)
  KR2: Month-over-month growth ≥ 20% (target: 20%)
  KR3: Day-30 retention ≥ 15% (target: 15%)

Objective: Build scalable growth engine
  KR1: CAC payback period < 12 months (target: <12mo)
  KR2: LTV/CAC ratio ≥ 3x (target: 3.0)
  KR3: Establish 2 profitable acquisition channels (target: 2)
```

### Series A (18-36 months)
```yaml
Objective: Scale revenue predictably
  KR1: Reach $100K MRR (target: $100K)
  KR2: Net revenue retention ≥ 110% (target: 110%)
  KR3: Gross margin ≥ 70% (target: 70%)

Objective: Build world-class team
  KR1: Hire 5 key roles (target: 5)
  KR2: Employee NPS ≥ 60 (target: 60)
  KR3: 90-day new hire retention ≥ 90% (target: 90%)
```
